Instructor and mentor - Vasilika Klimova

Vasilika Klimova is an author of several courses, collaborating for many years as an instructor and mentor, in well-known online and offline schools, such as HTMLAcademy, Netology, Moscow Coding School, GeekBrains school and Loftschool. Also she is a regular speaker at different Frontend Europe Conferences.

Vasilika has a unique ability to make complicated things easy, explaining technical material in a way that is clear to everyone. She has converted many of her students that started to learn coding from zero to change their job and to become a programmer, entering the exciting world of Information technology.
